About Moshly

Moshly is a Contrastdetails Lda. brand, founded by music professionals — a tight-knit team of tour managers, event producers and engineers who built tools to solve the real problems they faced on the road. What began as practical, in-house solutions quickly drew attention from colleagues; that momentum turned our tools into a product.
Today Moshly is a growing suite of focused apps designed to streamline the day-to-day work of music professionals.
