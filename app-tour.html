Moshly.io

The Central Ecosystem

More than just a landing page, Moshly.io is the backbone of your professional music career. It provides a unified identity system (Moshly ID), secure workspace management, and seamless integration between all micro-apps.

Managing a music project involves dozens of moving parts. Moshly.io consolidates your team, your data, and your tools into one high-performance hub designed for speed and clarity.

FeeME

The Smart Budget Generator

Stop fighting with spreadsheets. FeeME is built for those high-pressure moments when you need to generate a professional budget in minutes, not hours. Whether you're in the studio or backstage, FeeME lets you input expenses fast and see your margins immediately.

With one-click PDF generation and real-time visualization, you can send professional offers before the opportunity disappears. Cash in, and enjoy the rest of your day.

"FeeME turned my 2-hour budgeting nightmare into a 5-minute coffee break. It's an essential part of my touring toolkit."

MerchClick

Click Your Merch In

Inventory management shouldn't be a chore. MerchClick is designed for the chaotic environment of a merch table. Track every sale, inventory adjustment, and restock with simple, tactile "clicks".

Get instant reports on your best-sellers, real-time stock alerts, and clear performance data. No more post-show spreadsheet stress — just accurate data so you can focus on the music.

"We used to lose track of boxes every tour. With MerchClick, we know exactly where every shirt is at any moment."
